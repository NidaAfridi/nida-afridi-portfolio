Background

I think in systems — and I build them with people in mind.

I grew up in Kishanganj, Bihar, and I'm finishing a B.Tech in Computer Science & Communication Engineering at KIIT University. Somewhere along the way I noticed that the businesses doing well weren't the ones with the cleverest ideas — they were the ones who'd actually written the plan down. That's the work I gravitated toward: requirements gathering, process mapping, turning a stakeholder's half-formed need into a BRD someone can actually build from.

I'm also the person who ends up designing the deck, editing the video, or running broadcast for the college fest — so the analytical side and the creative side aren't separate for me. They're the same skill pointed at different problems.

Structure first, story second — but never just one.
